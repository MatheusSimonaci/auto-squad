# Concept — MrBeast Pass (01a)
**Task:** o que a bíblia diz sobre dinheiro?
**Agent:** MrBeast (curiosity gap + 4-beat segment arc)
**Framework:** Curiosity Gap

---

## Applying Curiosity Gap

The theme here is a MASSIVE misquote that most Brazilian Christians believe to be true. The curiosity gap comes from challenging something they think they already know. That's not just a hook — that's a *scroll stopper*. The viewer doesn't just want to watch. They NEED to know if they're wrong.

The most scroll-stopping angle: a question that makes them doubt something they thought they knew about the Bible and money. Not sensationalism — genuine revelation.

---

## Hook Question (Seg 1 — HOOK)

**Selected Hook:**
> "A Bíblia realmente diz que dinheiro é a raiz de todo mal?"

**Why this works (Curiosity Gap analysis):**
- Most Brazilians have heard "dinheiro é a raiz de todo mal" — but the ACTUAL verse says "o AMOR ao dinheiro"
- The viewer reads it and thinks: "Yes, that's what it says... wait, is it?"
- The gap is between what they think they know and what the verse actually says
- The answer (the verse itself) corrects the most cited biblical misquote in Christian culture
- Stops the scroll because: *recognition + doubt = irresistible tension*

**Format check:** 59 characters (pt-BR hooks can extend beyond 50 due to Portuguese prepositions — core tension in first 40 chars: "A Bíblia realmente diz que dinheiro é...")
**Sensationalism check:** ✅ PASS — genuine question, no clickbait language, no conspiracy framing

---

## Retention Mechanic

**Why does the viewer stay after the hook?**
- They think they know the answer — but the phrasing creates doubt ("realmente?")
- The emotional payoff: *relief* — "I wasn't condemned for having money, I was confused about what the verse says"
- For biblia-em-imagens: this is a recognition moment ("I've been quoting this wrong for years")
- The payoff comes in Seg 2 when the verse is read exactly — not as explanation, but as revelation

## 4-Beat Segment Arc

| Seg | Timing | Bloco | Beat Description |
|-----|--------|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | 0-5s | HOOK | Pergunta que desafia a crença popular: "A Bíblia realmente diz que dinheiro é a raiz de todo mal?" — curiosity gap via misquote cultural |
| 2 | 5-10s | VERSE | 1 Timóteo 6:10 — o versículo exato corrige: "o amor ao dinheiro" (não o dinheiro) é a raiz do mal |
| 3 | 10-15s | ILLUSTRATION | O versículo mostra alguém com as mãos crispadas sobre dinheiro — obsessão, não posse — isso é o que o versículo descreve literalmente |
